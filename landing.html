📖 What is Catechism Quest?

~ Unsa ni? ~

A free, traditional Catholic catechism app for children — built with love by a Filipino Catholic teacher (Ate MJ) for the families and students she serves.

Every section is in English AND Bisaya, so children growing up in the Philippines can hear the Faith in their mother tongue. Designed with care for traditional Catholic families — pre-Vatican II catechetics, the Traditional Latin Mass, the saints, and the Blessed Mother.

💫 Start with the Big Questions

Every child wonders: Who am I? Why am I here? Why is my heart beating?

The most important section in the app addresses these questions head-on — gently, in a child's voice, in the language of the soul searching for its Maker.

~ The road home, placed in a child's hands while they are still small enough to receive it. ~

📱 How to Install

You can install this app on your phone like a regular app — without going through the Play Store!

📱 On Android (Chrome):

1. Open the app → 2. Tap the menu (⋮) → 3. Tap "Install app" or "Add to Home Screen"

📱 On iPhone (Safari):

1. Open the app in Safari → 2. Tap the Share button (□↑) → 3. Tap "Add to Home Screen"
